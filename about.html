About Oreo

Oreo is a playful Parti Yorkshire Terrier with a big personality packed into a very small body. Full of energy and curiosity, he turns everyday moments into entertainment, whether he’s chasing his favorite toy, searching for snacks, or simply demanding attention like the star he is. With his distinctive coat and expressive face, Oreo doesn’t just exist in the room he owns it. His charm, intelligence, and endless enthusiasm make him more than just a pet; he’s a constant source of joy and the heart of the Oreo-verse.

Why I Created This Project

The Oreo-verse was created as a way to combine my love for my dog with my journey into software engineering. As I learn how to build websites, I wanted a project that felt personal and exciting rather than something generic. Creating a digital world for Oreo allows me to practice coding, explore my creativity, and build something meaningful at the same time. This project represents both my growth as a developer and the special bond I have with my dog, turning learning into something fun, intentional, and uniquely mine.
