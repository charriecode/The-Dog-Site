Meet Oreo

Meet Oreo, the king of the Oreo-verse. Though small in stature, this playful Parti Yorkshire Terrier carries a personality that fills every corner of the room. Like many Yorkies, Oreo is endlessly curious, spirited, and bursting with energy, always ready to investigate a new sound, chase a toy, or insert himself into whatever adventure the day might bring. Beneath his fluffy coat and expressive eyes is a clever little companion who knows exactly how to charm the people around him. Oreo also possesses one of life’s most refined canine passions: snack time. Treats are, without question, a highlight of his day especially when a generous family member offers the occasional tiny taste of human food. In those moments, Oreo’s excitement is unmistakable, and his enthusiasm reminds everyone around him that the simplest pleasures are often the most delightful.
